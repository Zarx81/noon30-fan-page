About Noon30 Catch Up

Jillian, Tiffany, and Amy are three longtime friends in their forties who know how to keep it real — and ridiculously funny.

Each week, they catch up, laugh hard, and talk candidly about whatever’s going on in their world. From weird news to wild opinions, no topic is too off-limits (or offbeat).

If you’ve ever wanted to eavesdrop on a group chat that’s equal parts hilarious and heartfelt, Noon30 Catch Up is your vibe.
